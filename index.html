About Us

Help the Neediest (HTN) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children through education, shelter, healthcare, and community support. We believe that every child deserves a future filled with hope and opportunity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower underprivileged children and families through sustainable support, resources, and education to create long-term positive impact.

What We Need

  • Hostel building materials
  • Library books and shelves
  • Playground equipment
  • Stationery and school supplies
  • Food and hygiene kits

Our Goal

To build a full resource center that provides a home, library, and recreational space for children in need, while equipping them with tools to thrive.
