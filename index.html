About Us

Help the Neediest (HTN) is a charitable organization founded by Twumasi Samuel. Our mission is to provide essential support and long-term solutions to the most vulnerable members of our society—especially children—through education, shelter, healthcare, and mentoring programs.

Our Mission

To empower impoverished children and families by giving them access to basic human needs and the tools to build a self-sufficient future. We believe in compassion, integrity, and lasting change.

What We Need

  • Construction of a children's hostel
  • Establishment of a library and study center
  • Playground and recreational facilities
  • Healthcare kits and nutritional food
  • School supplies, books, uniforms, and shoes
  • Volunteer mentors and teachers
