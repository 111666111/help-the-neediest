What We Need

Your donation helps us provide a safe, nurturing, and empowering space for vulnerable children. Here are some of our current needs:

  • Hostel: Safe and secure accommodation for children
  • Library: Books and learning materials to promote education
  • Playground: Space for play and physical development
  • School Supplies: Uniforms, backpacks, notebooks, pens
  • Food & Water: Nutritious meals and clean drinking water
  • Medical Kits: Basic health supplies and hygiene kits
  • Furniture: Beds, chairs, and desks for comfort and learning
  • Technology: Computers and internet for digital learning
  • Entertainment: Toys, games, and tools for creative play
