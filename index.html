About Us

Help the Neediest (HTN) is a non-profit organization committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children through education, shelter, life skills training, and healthcare. Our goal is to build safe, nurturing environments for those in desperate need of support.

Our Mission

To transform the lives of the most disadvantaged children through holistic care, empowering them to become leaders in their communities.

Our Work in Action

Our Goal

To establish a fully equipped center that includes a hostel, library, playground, and skill-training facilities to serve and uplift our beneficiaries.

What We Need

  • Books and educational materials
  • Construction materials for the hostel and library
  • Playground equipment
  • Clothing and hygiene products
  • Funds for food, healthcare, and training
