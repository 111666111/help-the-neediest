About Us

Help the Neediest is a growing non-profit organization driven by compassion and dedication to ensuring every child has the right to education, safety, and a bright future.

Founded with a vision to make a real difference, we work to uplift vulnerable children in Liberia and beyond by providing education, skills training, healthcare, and essential care.

Our Vision

  • Build a modern educational facility
  • Create a community library to inspire minds
  • Establish a safe playground for joy and development
  • Open hostels to provide safe shelter for children in need
