About Us

Help the Neediest is a growing non-profit organization driven by compassion and dedication to ensuring every child has the right to education, safety, and a bright future. We work to uplift vulnerable children in Liberia and beyond by providing education, skills training, healthcare, and essential care.

Our Programs

  • Building a modern educational facility
  • Creating a community library
  • Establishing a safe playground
  • Opening hostels for children in need
